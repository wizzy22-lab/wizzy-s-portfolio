8. Tier 2 — research-finding · criteria-list · before-after

Approach section dependencies. All max-widths per Section 6.

8.1 research-finding (1080px max, borderless · base structure)

[ SECONDARY RESEARCH ]

HVAC operators rely on tribal knowledge not systems

Across 14 maintenance teams, decisions repeated weekly but criteria stayed in operators' heads — never written, never shared, never accumulated.

8.1b research-finding compound (__media-grid · __sub-heading · __list · __paragraph + ds-media-placeholder)

[ 02.1 — SECONDARY RESEARCH ]

Analyzing community data revealed recurring patterns

FIG. 02.1.1

Community data sample 1

operator/approach/secondary-research-01.jpg

FIG. 02.1.2

Community data sample 2

operator/approach/secondary-research-02.jpg

Pattern

  • Constant adjustment driven by anxiety from lacking criteria
  • Emotional conflict between comfort and cost
  • Store-specific operations causing stress, leading to abandonment
  • Cost-saving behavior focused only on manual control

Observation

Operators were under stress balancing cost and comfort. They wanted to maintain a comfortable store environment while minimizing energy costs — and were visibly wrestling with how to operate the system to achieve both.

9. Tier 3 — split-feature · insight · media

Supporting / finishing components.

9.1 split-feature --50-50 --media-left --dark · Solution Feature

[ FEATURE 01 ]

One screen — judge and choose together

Cost projection, comfort history, and selection collapse into a single round-card so operators don't context-switch.

  • Real-time cost projection
  • Historical comfort data
  • One-tap selection
결과 / OUTCOME

Decisions made in under 10 seconds

9.2 split-feature --60-40 --media-right --dark · Iteration

[ ITERATION 01 ]

First prototype hid the decision behind a modal

Operators dismissed it without reading. We pulled criteria into the round itself, and engagement doubled the next week.

9.3 split-feature --50-50 --media-right --cream · Contrast

[ FEATURE 02 ]

Criteria live where the work happens

Surface team-agreed criteria inline so the next operator starts from context, not a blank slate.

9.4 insight --cream + --yellow (560px max)

[ INSIGHT ]

The work isn't the log — it's the conversation that didn't happen.

When criteria stay in heads, the next operator starts from zero. Surface them and the round becomes the meeting.

[ INSIGHT ]

A field tool earns its place by getting out of the way.

Operators tolerate 8 seconds of friction per round, not more. Above that, they invent a workaround.

9.4b insight --dark + .ds-need-pain-grid (Need vs Pain side-by-side)

USER NEED

Stay comfortable while cutting cost

PAIN POINT

Adjustments lack cost feedback — anxiety follows
